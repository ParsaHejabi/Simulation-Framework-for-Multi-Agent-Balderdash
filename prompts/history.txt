The history of your performance in previous rounds is provided in csv format and following is the column descriptions:
Word: the announced word.
Definition: the dictionary definition of the announced word.
Generated_Definition: your definiton for that round.
Winning Definition: definiton provided by the winner of that round.
Writing_True_Definition: boolean showing whehter you have written down the correct definiton.
Guessing_Correct_Definiton: boolean showing whether you have guessed correctly in voting phase.
Deception_Score: the number of players that were deceived by your definiton in voting phase.
